Pizza recipe!


This recipe is will make great tasting pizza. It will be the only pizza recipe you will need to impess your family and friends

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • red sauce
  • pepperoni
  • salt

Steps

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease two 12-inch pizza pans.
  2. Step 2 Make sauce: Whisk together water, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Step 3 Make crust: Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  4. Step 4 Transfer dough to a floured surface; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes. Add more flour as needed. (If using RapidRise yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)
  5. Step 5 Divide dough in half. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prepared pizza pans.
